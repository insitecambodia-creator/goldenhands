Our Story

A craft passed down
through Reaksa's family

Long before Golden Hands became a name on the riverside, the practice of Khmer massage was already a family inheritance. Reaksa grew up around it, and today she personally selects and trains every therapist who works here, so that each pair of hands carries the same discipline and care as her own.

The spa was recently renovated into a calmer, more considered space, one built for people who want their hour of stillness to feel unhurried. We welcome the residents of Kampot as warmly as the travelers passing through it, in a room designed to feel like neither a hotel spa nor a roadside massage shop, but something in between: familiar, professional, and genuinely restful.

Apsara Four Hands Massage

Two therapists, one unbroken rhythm. Inspired by the grace of Apsara dance, this treatment layers four hands in careful synchrony, a deeper, fuller release than a single pair of hands can offer. It is the treatment guests request when they want the whole hour, and themselves, taken care of completely.
