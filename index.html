About Us

Relaxation, Cambodian Style

Golden Hands Massage sits in the heart of Kampot, a short walk from the riverside promenade. We bring together generations-old Khmer massage traditions with the quiet, unhurried pace of this small river town — the perfect place to slow down after exploring the pepper farms, the old French quarter or Bokor Mountain.

Every therapist on our team is locally trained and experienced, using natural oils, warm herbal compresses and time-tested techniques to relieve tension, improve circulation and restore balance to body and mind.
